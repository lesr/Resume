Run/Master - JCL rerun restart automation
This software provides the means for automatically restarting a failed job, accounting for all MVS catalog maintenance, dataset maintenance, and GDG bias adjustments for job reruns. Tape/2000 - tape management
Monitors, manages, and records all tape processing, real-time, assigning and providing overwrite protection based on user defined criteria. There were no hard modifications to the OS. OPEN/CLOSE/EOV modules were modified, (in memory), at subsystem startup. Ageing and offsite cycling, for disaster recovery, was supported. TAPE2000 allows users to account for every tape in your datacenter, whether it is in your library or at a disaster recovery/offsite vault.
